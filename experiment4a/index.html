Experiment 4a - Feast of Plenty

Description

This is a generator for an infinite table with food for you to eat.

Technical

I used the p5 drawing tools and lists with randomness to provide you with infinite food.

Reflection

This was a fun task because I like the idea of infinite free food. I had a good time trying to figure out how to get the plates to appear above the tile. I took inspiration from the example game "You will never be king" in order to draw the plates. I am really proud of the always refilling plates with fruits on them as well.
